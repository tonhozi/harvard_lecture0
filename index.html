About me

Hi, my name is Pedro Tonhozi de Oliveira. This is a website about my research and publications. Here are some text about myself from my LinkedIn profile that you can access .

Pedro Tonhozi de Oliveira is an entrepreneurship professor over Fundação Getúlio Vargas. He also works as CEO at Innova Brazil, a consulting company for startups and CTO at Jurinove, an legal practice software. He works as a management & engineering consultant with expertise in startup finance, writing a winning business plan, due diligence, strategic planning, new market expansion, international entrepreneurship, new product development, project management, re-engineering, and operations.

Prior, he was a Visiting Assistant Professor of Entrepreneurship and Innovation at Western Kentucky University and a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Global Entrepreneurship and Innovation at the University of Missouri-Kansas City's Henry W. Bloch School of Management. He earned a Ph.D. in Entrepreneurship & Innovation and Telecommunications & Computer Networking from the University of Missouri-Kansas City in 2017; and a Bachelorâ€™s in Electrical Engineering from the Federal University of Parana in Brazil in 2011. Before entering academia, he worked as a project manager at an Internet Service Provider and Oil & Gas company developing IT infrastructure solutions.

Pedro's expertise spans the entrepreneurship field, focusing on entrepreneurial crowdfunding and finance, new product development, Bayesian, web data collection, and big data. He has publications on top FT50 journals like AMJ, ET&P, and JBV. He has presented his research all over the world in top conferences including RENT, Babson, Academy of International Business, SEE, and the Academy of Management. He also is the chair for the most important Brazilian Research Conference outside Brazil - Brascon Conference. He is fluent in English, Portuguese, and German, and intermediate in Spanish.
